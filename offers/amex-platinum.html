The American Express Platinum Card is one of the strongest premium travel cards available if you actually use the benefits. Best For

The Amex Platinum is best for frequent travelers, luxury hotel users, airport lounge users, and people who can realistically use the card’s travel and lifestyle credits. If you travel often and know how to maximize statement credits, this card can easily justify its annual fee.
