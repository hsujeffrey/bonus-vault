Best For

This card is best for frequent travelers, luxury travelers, airport lounge users, and anyone who can realistically use the various statement credits throughout the year. If you only travel occasionally, it may be difficult to justify the annual fee.

Things to Know

  • The annual fee is $895.
  • Many benefits require enrollment.
  • Several credits are distributed monthly, quarterly, or semi-annually.
  • The best value comes from frequent travel and maximizing the available credits.
  • Welcome bonus offers may vary by applicant.
